The Artist.

Francesco Cucci was born in Lecce in 1948. He currently lives and works in Varese. His style reflects the multiple facets of his personality and creative approaches, characterized by a deep sensitivity for matter and interests in anthropology and architecture. These elements merge with a rigorous and tireless stylistic and conceptual research.

His works stand out for the audacity to challenge classic canons, while maintaining a lucid and profound historical rigor. The artist's multifaceted creativity generates a dynamic and passionate dialogue between a wide range of expressive forms and contemporary artistic tendencies, always pushing to open new perspectives.

His unmistakable creations suggest clear progress towards new architectural and compositional structures. All elements of his work (form, space, matter, light, and color) are never separated but converge and develop into a single, compact whole. Form and content come to invade the surrounding space, transforming the observer into a protagonist.
